# Superstore Sales Analysis Project

## Project Overview

This project focuses on cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing Superstore sales data using Python and Power BI. The objective was to extract meaningful business insights, identify revenue trends, analyze top-performing products, and create an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

---

# Objectives

* Clean and preprocess raw sales data
* Analyze sales and profit trends
* Identify top-selling products and categories
* Evaluate regional business performance
* Create an interactive Power BI dashboard
* Generate business insights and recommendations

---

# Data Cleaning Steps

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

1. Removed duplicate records
2. Checked and handled missing values
3. Converted date columns into datetime format
4. Created additional time-based columns:

   * Year
   * Month
   * Month Name
5. Standardized dataset structure for analysis

---

# Exploratory Data Analysis

## Revenue Trend Analysis

* Monthly sales trends were analyzed to identify seasonal patterns.
* Sales spikes were observed during certain months.

## Top-Selling Products

* High-value products contributed a major percentage of total revenue.
* Technology products dominated overall sales.

## Category Analysis

* Technology category generated the highest revenue.
* Furniture and Office Supplies also performed strongly.

## Regional Analysis

* West region achieved the highest sales.
* South region showed comparatively lower performance.

---

# Key Insights

* Technology products are the primary revenue drivers.
* Seasonal demand significantly affects monthly sales.
* Few products contribute disproportionately to total revenue.
* Regional performance varies significantly.
* Strategic marketing can improve underperforming regions.

---

# Business Recommendations

## Growth Strategy

* Focus on high-performing technology products.
* Improve regional marketing in low-performing areas.
* Introduce customer retention programs.
* Use seasonal promotions to maximize revenue.

## Operational Improvements

* Improve inventory planning during peak seasons.
* Use customer segmentation for targeted campaigns.
* Bundle complementary products to increase average order value.
